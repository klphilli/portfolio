Build Your Skincare Routine

After figuring out your skin type. It is important to develop and understand what is needed for a basic skin care routine. Below is a breif guide to what is needed morning and night. Finding products and a routine that works for you is guaranteed to have your skin flourishing. Always remember to do your research and keep these steps in mind. Cleanse. Treat. Protect.

Cleanser AM/PM.

Toner AM and Chemical Exfoliant (AHA/BHA) 2-3x /week PM

Vitamin C AM

Retinol 2-3x /week PM

Masks 2-3x /week PM

Serum depending on product type AM/PM

Mosturizer AM/PM

SPF AM
