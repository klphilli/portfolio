Build Your Skincare Routine

After figuring out your skin type. It is important to develop and understand what is needed for a basic skin care routine. Below is a breif guide to what is needed morning and night. Finding products and a routine that works for you is guaranteed to have your skin flourishing. Always remember to do your research and keep these steps in mind. Cleanse. Treat. Protect.

Cleanser AM/PM. There are many different types of cleansers that includes: Gel, Cream, Foam, Oil/Balm.

Toner AM and Chemical Exfoliant (AHA/BHA) 2-3x /week PM

Vitamin C AM

Retinol 2-3x /week PM

Masks 2-3x /week PM There are many different types of masks that includes: Clay, Cream, Gel, Sheet, Exfoliating.

Serum depending on product type AM/PM There are many different types of serums that includes: Anti-Aging, Skin Brightening, Acne Fighting, Hydrating, Exfoliating, Repairitive and Renewing.

Mosturizer AM/PM 3 main types of mosturizer includes: Emollients, humectants, occlusives

SPF AM spf coverage ranges the higher the better
